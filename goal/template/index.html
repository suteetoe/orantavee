At Orantavee Co., LTD. our customers come to us from all over the nation for quality engine parts. We have over 30+ years of industry experience and expertise in engine parts. We take pride on building good relationships with our customers, and take the time to understand any engine issues presented to us and recommend only the appropriate replacement parts. Our extensive product range includes high quality Japanese parts to suit all budgets.

Experties

We specialize in heavy duty trucks, pickup trucks, agricultural tractors, sport utility vehicles, passenger vehicles, compressors and power generators.

Brand

Maruboshi

Maruboshi gaskets are produced according to OEM standards using a sophisticated process that provides a precise, determined compression. This ensures a perfect seal every time.

Guez

Guez engine gaskets are manufactured from the highest quality materials and feature a superior design that performs under pressure and provides a long lasting seal for heavy duty vehicles. The full set consists of every gasket component the technician needs to overhaul cylinder heads.

Nippon Ota

Nippon Ota gaskets feature an unique collection of enhancements that create a stronger combustion seal. This ensures superior performance and longevity under the toughest conditions. Nippon Ota has a wide range of specialties for commercial and non-commercial vehicle engine models to choose from.

Tonyco

Tonyco specialised in producing the best quality diesel gaskets for trucks, buses and heavy equipment. Our products are made by the high quality materials imported from Japan and the USA according to OEM standards and pass our strictest quality controls.

Top

Top's pistons are designed with sophisticated technology and best in class architecture to withstand extreme conditions. This results in the strength and toughness that only high quality pistons can provide. These pistons feature gallery cooling and anodizing coating where necessary. Top offers a wide range of pistons for both light and heavy-duty vehicles. Their piston kit consists of everything a technician needs to complete a rebuild.

Suisun

Suisun provides best in-class pistons, designed using leading technology to ensure outstanding quality and performance. Suisun offers wide a variety of products for nearly every popular vehicle and application in the market.

Omiya

Omiya provides excellent quality wet and dry cylinder liners. Omiya liners are manufactured using the best materials to deliver superior bonding, reduced distortion, increased oil retention. Outer chrome plating is used to prevent cavitation. Omiya offers an extensive range of liners for all type of engines, tractors, cars, commercial vehicles and generators.

Chromed

Chromed cylinder liners are manufactured using high quality materials to provide wear & corrosion resistance for optimum durability. Chromed cylinders are designed to provide the best performance in extreme running conditions. The Chromed product range is tailored for light trucks and mid-range commercial vehicles.

We specialize in heavy duty trucks, pickup trucks, agricultural tractors, sport utility vehicles, passenger vehicles, compressors and power generators.
